How It Works

Response in Seconds,
Not Minutes

The SKPS system is designed to act faster than any human could — fully automatic and always on.

01

Detection

Gas sensor continuously monitors air quality. The moment gas concentration exceeds the safety threshold, it sends a signal to the PLC instantly.

02

PLC Processing

The Programmable Logic Controller receives the signal and evaluates the threat level, triggering the appropriate response protocol in under 500ms.

03

Gas Shutoff

Both solenoid valves close simultaneously, cutting gas supply to the kitchen and preventing further fuel from feeding any potential ignition source.

04

Alarm & Ventilation

Audible and visual alarms activate to alert occupants. If the optional ventilation module is installed, exhaust fans activate to clear the space.

05

Suppression Ready

If fire is detected — or if the gas ignites — the automatic fire extinguishing system discharges, suppressing flames before they spread.

06

Safe Reset

Once the area is cleared and declared safe, a technician or authorized user performs a manual reset, restoring the system to its armed standby state.
